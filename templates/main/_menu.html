Welcome to the orcagame!

In this study, we have a few different games that you can play in which you listen to orca calls. In playing the games, you will be using your human intelligence to solve problems that are difficult for computers but easy for humans. With the classifications you make in these games, you will be helping scientists to better understand orca communication.

  • Orca/Background/Voice matching game
  • Call matching game
  • User survey
