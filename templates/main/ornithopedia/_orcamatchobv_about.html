In this game you are asked to label an unknown clip of audio from the Openmir with one of the following labels: Background or boat noise, Orca, Distant Orca, Voice note, Unknown call or Skip. This is shown below. When you label a call, it will flash briefly, and then you will be shown the next call. You can also navigate backwards and forwards though the interface with the arrows at the top right corner.

The classifications that you make are very valuable to our research, this is a task that is very hard for computers to do on their own, but with your labels, we can train the computer to eventually automatically find the orca calls in the vast archive of sound files we have in the Openmir.
