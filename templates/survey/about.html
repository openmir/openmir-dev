We want to make our games better and to do this, we need your feedback. After playing the games, we would love it if you would answer a short user survey and tell us about your experiences with our game. This short survey will ask you to rate different aspects of the game on a 5 point scale and is completely voluntary.

The purpose of this research project is to design mobile and web based interfaces and casual games to engage citizen scientists to listen to, see, and interact with the vocalizations of orcas (killer whales), birdsong and partially annotated chants. We will attempt to design these casual mini-games so that they are engaging and fun for our participants. The data collected from these games will be used to help train machine learning and machine hearing systems to help us to classify these vocalizations.
